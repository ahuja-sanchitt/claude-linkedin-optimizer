Based on the new positioning, generate a 4-week LinkedIn content plan to build authority BEFORE any cold outreach.

**Structure:**
- Week 1: 3 builder-credibility posts (something shipped, learned, or built).
- Week 2: 3 lessons-from-current-role posts (specific, slightly contrarian).
- Week 3: 3 POV posts on their domain (a take they would defend in an interview).
- Week 4: 3 behind-the-scenes posts (what the day-to-day actually looks like).

**For each of the 12 posts, give:**
- Hook (first line, must work on mobile, must stop a scroll).
- Body angle (3–5 lines of structure, not the full post).
- Format (text post, carousel, document upload, short video).
- The ONE specific question to end with to drive comments.

Sequence them so they compound. By the end of week 4, a recruiter checking the activity feed should see a coherent narrative about who the person is and what they build.
